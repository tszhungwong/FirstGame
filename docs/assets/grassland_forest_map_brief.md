## Style

- East-Asian fantasy accents such as subtle curved edging, cloud motifs, carved stones, or small warm-metal details; keep them restrained and functional.
- Natural grass green, deep forest green, river blue, and earth brown; control large highly saturated areas.
- Similar overall density and finish to a polished mobile arena map.

## Prohibitions
